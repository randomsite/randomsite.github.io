CRAZY MAZE

Welcome to Crazy Maze! Crazy Maze is a set of absolutely confusing, intimidating and bizarre mazes on Scratch. They aren't just any ordinary mazes. If you touch the black, you lose! There are also portals that transport you to a different place. But beware, for there may be some false portals! I challenge you to complete ALL 5 levels. Let's see what you got!


Level 1: Confusing Crossway

Level 2: ZigZagger

Level 3: FishTrap


A tribute to fish!

Level 4: Force Field Mania


In this level, there are invisible force fields. If you touch one, you are out! The red dots signify the starting and ending of a force field. Some may be broken, however!

Level 5: The Shifting Plates


This maze contains moving obstacles! If you touch the purple, you get boosted towards the right. This could help, but in some areas it just makes it harder! You have 3 lives in this maze. You are truly a master if you complete this.
